Shipping, Handling, and Ordering

Three ways to get your Big Skinny:
  1. The fastest and easiest way to shop: on our secure, efficient website. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit cards as well as Paypal. Our YAHOO Merchant site features all current Security Features. NOTE: Orders ship from New Jersey, USA
  2. Call Toll Free 1.617.500.6939 to place your order with one of our knowledgeable staff. Please note that the Big Skinny phone is answered between Monday through Friday 8am- 10pm EST
Shipping & Handling Costs:
  • USPS First Class (Trackable): $3.95 - shipping & handling via 1st class mail ($.50 per additional wallet) for USA. Regular mail orders usually arrive within 5-10 business days in the USA unless we are slammed with another snowstorm which delays everything. Please note that items going to Hawaii and Alaska often take about 3-5 days longer than items shipped to the continental US. And don't forget, you lucky birdy, the US Post Office delivers on Saturdays!
  • USPS Priority Mail: $8.00 - USPS won't guarantee priority mail shipping but they are usually pretty good at delivering within 2-3 days.
  • USPS International International Overseas Shipping & Handling costs $20.00 shipped via Airmail which usually arrive within 7-14+ business days. Note: if your country has a slow mail service, then packages may take longer.
  • Promotions and/or coupons. Yes, once in a while we have those and if you are on our email list, you'll be the first to know. We will only accept one coupon or promotional code at once--they cannot be combined. And due to the volume of orders, we cannot combine multiple promo or coupon orders in the same package even if they are going to the same address so you might end up with multiple gifties to joyously open.
